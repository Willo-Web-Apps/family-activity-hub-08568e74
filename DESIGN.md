# Design DNA — Rallyday

## Visual Personality: warm-luxurious
## Layout: community
## Typography: classic-balanced

### Color Palette
- Primary: #2C5F8A → oklch(0.42 0.09 230) — confident deep blue
- Accent: #F0824A → oklch(0.67 0.16 40) — energetic warm orange
- Surface: #FDF7F2 → oklch(0.98 0.01 65) — warm cream
- Text: oklch(0.22 0.015 230) — deep blue-warm charcoal

### Font Pairing
- Display: IBM Plex Serif — weights 400, 600, 700 (warmth, editorial authority)
- Body: IBM Plex Sans — weights 400, 500, 600 (clean, professional, readable)

### Unique Design Choices
- Hero uses layered warm cream-to-orange radial gradients with a grain texture overlay — feels like a Saturday morning vibe, NOT a cold sports app
- Impact stats displayed in warm orange accent cards with generous rounded corners and serif numerals — conveys energy and warmth simultaneously
- Section backgrounds alternate between warm cream (#FDF7F2) and soft warm white with a bottom-edge wave SVG divider between hero and stats — no harsh borders
- Testimonial cards use a large decorative serif quotation mark in accent orange — human, personal, magazine-quality
- CTA section uses the deep blue as a rich background with warm cream text and an orange CTA button — confident but inviting
